# Future Ideas

This file records possibilities, not commitments.

Ideas belong here until they are justified by real extraction work and promoted through an Architecture Decision Record (ADR).


- Validation of canonical JSON and required provenance.
- Deterministic diagram generation.
- Cross-reference and impact analysis across facts.
- Search and local indexes derived entirely from canonical files.
- Comparison of factory configurations and vehicle overlays.
- Review reports for ambiguity, conflicting sources, or missing applicability.
- Import helpers for tables, diagrams, and repeated publication structures.
- Export formats for repair planning, inspection, or educational views.
- Additional source types such as revisions and service bulletins.
- Graph consistency and integrity checking beyond schema validation.

Potential tools, including AI-assisted extraction and reasoning, may propose or locate
information. They must remain replaceable consumers or assistants around the
graph. They do not become authorities, and their output does not bypass
provenance or review.

No future idea should delay **One Diagram** or force infrastructure into the
canonical knowledge model.
